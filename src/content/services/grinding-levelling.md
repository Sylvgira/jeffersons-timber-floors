A flawless timber floor starts with a perfectly prepared subfloor. We provide concrete grinding, surface preparation, and self-levelling compounds to create a smooth, level base ready for any timber flooring system.

## What is included

- Concrete grinding and surface profiling
- Self-levelling compound application
- Moisture barrier installation
- Crack repair and substrate assessment
- Surface tolerance testing

## Best fit

Renovations and new builds where the existing slab or subfloor needs preparation before timber flooring can be installed. Essential for achieving a level, stable, long-lasting floor.
