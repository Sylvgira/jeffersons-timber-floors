## What is included

- Damage assessment and repair plan
- Board replacement and colour matching
- Structural fixes for subfloor issues
- Spot sanding and recoating
- Maintenance schedule recommendations

## Best fit

Homeowners and property managers who need targeted repairs rather than full restoration. Also ideal for rental properties and pre-sale preparation.
