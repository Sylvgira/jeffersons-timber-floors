Melbourne homes deserve floors that look as good as they feel underfoot. Our sanding and polishing service strips back years of wear and applies a flawless finish that protects and enhances the natural character of your timber.

- On-site assessment and colour matching

- Water-based or solvent-based finish options
- Final inspection and care instructions

This service suits homeowners and businesses with solid timber floors that need restoration — whether they're original Baltic pine, hardwood, or engineered boards.
