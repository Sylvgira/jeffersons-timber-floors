Every project starts with a conversation. Here's what to expect when you get in touch.

## 1. Enquiry and assessment

Contact us with a brief description of what you need. We'll arrange a site visit to inspect your floors, discuss options, and take measurements.

## 2. Quote and recommendations

Within a few days, you'll receive a detailed quote with our recommended approach, timeline, and finish options. No pressure, no hidden costs.

## 3. Booking and preparation

Once you're ready, we lock in dates that work for you. We'll advise on any preparation needed before we arrive.

## 4. The work

We arrive on time, protect your space, and get to work. Most residential jobs are completed within a few days.

## 5. Final walk-through

Before we leave, we walk through the finished result with you and provide care instructions to keep your floors looking their best.
